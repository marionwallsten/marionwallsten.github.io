About me

Hi! I’m Marion Wallsten, and I am about to finish my Bachelor’s in Computer Science at Uppsala University!
Before I started studying in Uppsala, I lived in London for 6 months working in retail, and I also studied at Santa Monica College for a year. I love learning about, and experience different cultures!
During my time at Uppsala University, I have been engaged with the Engineering and Science Students’ union, and have had different positions throughout the years. During my second year I was responsible for maintaining and updating the app and website for the reception of engineering and science students’, as well as making their first two weeks at the university memorable by being active in different activities.
In the spring of 2022 I was responsible for updating the website, as well as maintaining the registration system for the teams that participated in the River Rafting event that is held each year in Uppsala!
