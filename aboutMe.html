About me

Hi! I’m Marion Wallsten, and I am about to finish my Bachelor’s in Computer Science at Uppsala University!
I have since I grew up always found problem solving very enjoyable. When I was younger I loved playing puzzle video games, and it is still one of my favourite spare time activities. This interest has today translated to working on programming problems, which, for me, is kind of like a puzzle game! Technology has also always been an interest of mine, and I am always excited about learning about new technology, on my own, or as part of a team learning from others. I always want to evolve to be a better problem solver, programmer and person!
I am not afraid of working on different kinds of problems on my own, but I have learnt the value of teamwork, and the advantages of working together as a team after doing a lot of group projects in school, and also from working in the service industry. I aim to always deliver the best version of myself and my work, to grow, and at the same time contribute to other people growing and evolving too.
