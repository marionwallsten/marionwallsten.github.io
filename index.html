Webmaster for the reception of new engineering and science students (TD-mottagningen)
Student union of Engineering and Science students at Uppsala University (UTN) (Feb 2021 - Oct 2021)
During my second year at Uppsala University, I was responsible for updating, maintaining and launching the student union's mobile application for new students, which was built in React Native and used Firebase. The app was launched on both IOS and Android. The information on the app was tailored depending on what program the new student's belonged to. They could see their schedule, who was that particular programs closest contact, book tickets to events, aside from general information about the reception. A part from the app, I was also responsible for the website that acted as an alternative to the application. The application had about 1000 daily users during the reception. Before this project I had never worked with mobile applications, and only knew some basics in web development. This project took 400+ hours, distributed on several weekly meetings, planning, coding, and preperations for the reception. It spanned over six months and was done while being a full time student!
