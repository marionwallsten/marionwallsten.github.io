Webmaster for the reception of new engineering and science students (TD-mottagningen)
Union of Engineering and Science students at Uppsala University (UTN) (Feb 2021 - Oct 2021)
During my second year at Uppsala University, I was responsible for updating, maintaining and launching the student union's mobile application for new students, which was built in React Native and used Firebase. The app was launched on both iOS and Android. The information on the app was tailored depending on what program the new student's belonged to. They could see their schedule, who was that particular program's closest contact, book tickets to events, and view general information about the reception. Apart from the app, I was also responsible for the website that acted as an alternative to the application. The application had about 1000 daily users during the reception. Before this project I had never worked with mobile applications, and only knew some basics in web development. This project took 400+ hours, distributed on several weekly meetings, planning, coding, and preparations for the reception. It spanned over six months and was done while being a full time student!

Webmaster for Uppsalas River Rafting event (Forsränningen)
Union of Engineering and Science students at Uppsala University (UTN) (Nov 2021 - Oct 2022)
During the spring of 2022 I was responsible for updating and maintaining the website for the River Rafting event that is held each year in Uppsala. The website is the main information distribution place, and is used by not only students but also people from in and outside of Uppsala who want to come and see the River Rafting. This year there was an estimate of 80 000 spectators at the river rafting, and the event was also broadcasted on national TV. My role included designing the website, and making relevant updates about the event. I was also responsible for managing the sign up system for all of the student’s who wanted to participate, in 2022 we got over 800 applications for getting a raft! You can see the website here!
